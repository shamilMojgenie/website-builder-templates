About GLOBALCON 2025

GLOBALCON 2025 is an international summit dedicated to innovation, research, and multidisciplinary collaboration. This year's edition brings together professionals, educators, and researchers from across the world for a two-day immersive experience.

The conference features expert-led discussions, keynote sessions, hands-on workshops, and opportunities for cross-industry networking. Delegates can look forward to thought-provoking conversations, new perspectives, and sessions that highlight emerging trends across multiple fields.

A special highlight of GLOBALCON 2025 includes the unveiling of the incoming leadership panel for the upcoming year, along with a recognition ceremony honoring outstanding contributors within the global community.

Designed to balance academic rigor with an engaging event atmosphere, the conference offers a perfect platform for learning, collaboration, and meaningful professional growth. Registrations are now open—reserve your seat and be a part of this inspiring global meet.

Venue

Horizon Event Centre, Riverside City

The Horizon Event Centre is a modern, spacious venue located near the scenic riverside district of Riverside City.

Designed for both intimate gatherings and large-scale events, the venue offers multiple halls, open outdoor areas, and state-of-the-art facilities. The main indoor hall accommodates up to 2,000 guests comfortably.

With dedicated technical support, ample parking space, and a flexible layout, the Horizon Event Centre is ideal for conferences, exhibitions, and social functions of every scale.
