Welcome to MEDICON 2026

Greetings from the Organising Committee!

We are delighted to welcome you to the Annual Medical Sciences Conference, bringing together distinguished clinicians, researchers, and healthcare innovators from across the nation.

The event features keynote lectures, breakthrough research presentations, hands-on workshops, and opportunities for meaningful professional networking — all designed to elevate clinical excellence.

Hosted in the vibrant city of Chandigarh, the conference provides an inspiring environment to share knowledge, build collaborations, and shape the future of healthcare.

Medicon ’26: Advancing Medical Excellence

Medicon ’26 was founded by a group of senior medical professionals with the aim of bringing healthcare experts together and fostering meaningful exchange of ideas. The platform encourages collaboration, shared learning, and continuous growth across various medical specialties.

The conference emphasizes academic sessions, public awareness initiatives, and training-focused programs designed to support professional development throughout the healthcare community.

Medicon ’26 also features hands-on workshops, clinical case discussions, and interactive learning formats that help participants stay informed about the latest advancements. The initiative promotes knowledge exchange, skill enhancement, and excellence in modern medical practice.
