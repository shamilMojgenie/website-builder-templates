A Swipe, A Spark, A Forever

Eve and Adam's journey began with a simple swipe in March 2024 — one just starting out, the other ready to move on. But fate had plans of its own. Their first date was unassuming: a pizza, quiet laughter, and a rare sense of ease.

From long-distance calls and strolls through golden autumns to Broadway shows and boba dates, their bond grew with every shared moment. A turning point came in June — Eve’s White Coat Ceremony — when their story became one shared with family and future.

Our Love Story

THE PERFECT SWIPE

March 2024

Every love story begins somewhere — theirs started with a swipe on Hinge. Eve, new to the world of dating apps, matched with Adam, who was just about to delete his account. One was starting out, the other ready to move on — but fate clearly had other plans.

FIRST CONNECTION

March 2024

Their first date was quiet and unassuming: a shared pizza, easy conversation, and an instant comfort that felt rare. Adam was flying to Indiana the next day, but something about that evening lingered — a feeling that this was more than just a chance meeting.

MOMENTS TOGETHER

From long-distance calls and late-night texts to boba dates, strolls through golden Michigan autumns, Broadway shows, and even the occasional pickleball match — each moment brought them closer, stitched together by laughter and shared ease.

FOREVER BEGINS
